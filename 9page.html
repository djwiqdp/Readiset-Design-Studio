Readiset Studio is an experimental and creative design lab led by three designers who transcend boundaries, working across print to graphic and interactive media.

Reading a Line of Poetry in a Million Years of Solitude, pages 25, 43, and 201

148*210mm

I created a book collecting haikus from pages 25, 43, and 201 from the Japanese haiku book titled "Reading a Line of Poetry in a Million Years of Solitude." I utilized tracing paper to mimic the accumulation of snow as the pages turn, capturing the expression of snow that gradually blurs and covers, symbolizing the fleeting nature of life.
