Readiset Studio is an experimental and creative design lab led by three designers who transcend boundaries, working across print to graphic and interactive media.

World of Representationss

130*230mm

Objects are a "representation" of our lives, and through these representations, we understand the world and shape our own stories. This carries a philosophical message. Based on interviews about the interviewees' most cherished objects, the upper book contains the brand's objective representation of the objects, while the lower book captures the interviewees' subjective representations of the objects. Through this, the books explore how personal life goals and philosophies are reflected in these objects.
