Readiset Studio is an experimental and creative design lab led by three designers who transcend boundaries, working across print to graphic and interactive media.

45 Days in Vancouver

210*148mm

This book records the experiences and impressions of Jungyu Kim, who spent 45 days in Vancouver from early January to mid-February 2024, along with factual information. It offers a glimpse into the perspective of a man living in the metropolitan area of South Korea.
