Readiset Studio is an experimental and creative design lab led by three designers who transcend boundaries, working across print to graphic and interactive media.

Design Cheat Key

210*148mm

This book aims to develop unique experimental graphic forms through an exploration of typical and atypical methods, specific processes, and various influences. It contains various works that pretend to be the creations of famous designers, but in reality, they are produced through very simple processes. This methodology book introduces a design approach where each page the reader unfolds randomly applies a different process, incorporating the principles of a magic book.
