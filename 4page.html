Readiset Studio is an experimental and creative design lab led by three designers who transcend boundaries, working across print to graphic and interactive media.

If We Can't Go at the Speed of Light

148*210mm

The content about Louis's planet visually represents the form of language as seen from Muriin's (Louis) perspective by expressing it in color, allowing it to be understood in various ways. The design is implemented so that when the letters overlap and are covered with colored cellophane, they become visible. On the back, only the form of the color language is shown, depicting the world as Louis sees it in colors and shapes. Hee-jin collects glass to interpret the language of color from a strange planet, and continues this method until her return to Earth and her death. The interpretation technique using colored cellophane allows readers to experience it as well.
